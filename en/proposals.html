Proposals

When you want a relaxing moment, an unforgettable day, or you are looking for a special way to discover Tuscany, just try to imagine it.

My Fair Italy® will make your dream comes true.

  • Only for two
  • Driving a Ferrari
  • Flying on Tuscany
  • White truffle
  • Wine tasting and olive oil tasting tour
  • Cooking classes

Do you want a special declaration of love for you fiancé or celebrate a special occasion? Have you never thought about the special effect a romantic open space can generate in your partner? And if time is very short, it can be spent in a dinner in a romantic place for just your partner and you… Contact us and we will make your dream come true!

If you think that driving a Ferrari would be a dream that comes true…Contact us and ask for a tour Maranello, to visit the Ferrari Museum and to try one of their fantastic cars in a real circuit, with a real pilote! We will choose your accomodation too, in one of the best hotels in the surrounding… And you will just have to enjoy your magic trip.

Do you wish to experience the exclusive emotion of a flight over the Tuscan land? To discover from a very original point of view the secrets of Tuscan historical cities and the the Chianti vineyards? Thanks to the cooperation with the best operators of the zone you will fly on the most romantic mean of air-transport: The Hot Air Balloon! Then you can choose to end your trip with a wine tasting directly in the farm where it is produced.

Why don’t you try the experience of truffle hunting? San Miniato hills are famous for white truffles and we can organize a real truffle hunting for you with a real truffle hunter and his dog. Typical white truffle that grows in the region together with the black truffle is the most important underground treasure in Tuscany and in particular in San Miniato. Every year San Miniato celebrates a National Festival where people can taste a lot of truffle-based products as well as fresh truffles. We organize half-day truffle hunting tours: an experience for both your nose and your mouth! A local truffle hunter will wait for you and will bring you through the woods of the san miniato hills for a truffle hunting of about 2 hours together with his personal dogs. He will also explain the method to train properly a dog to hunt for truffle. It will be your unforgettable memory of Tuscany! On request it is possible to have a truffle-made products tasting after the hunting.

Tuscany is the wine country. Italians drink at lunch a little, and at dinner mostly, The wine is an everyday presence, like bread, pasta or olive oil. You know the wine by sampling and experiencing. So why do not experience this kind of activity directly in the farm where wines are producted? A private transfer will pick you up near your accommodation at the time agreed and will take you to the farm where you will spend the whole day or half day just enjoying nature and learning about wines and their production. You will get in touch with a real tuscan farm and the real farmers who work and live there.

The wine tour start meeting the farmer. This is very important in order to understand his philosophy and his way of producing wine. Then the visit to the vineyards. According to the season, you will experience the different phases of the farming and if you are in the right period of the year (summer-end or autumn-start) you will also taste the grapevine directly from the plants!

After you will visit the cellars were the grapes start the process to become wine, and you will be tought about all the phases from the grape harvest up to the bottling. Then, finally you will taste all the wines you have seen in the cellars together with tuscan bread, salami, olive oil and all the typical tuscan products. After the visit to the wine farm you will move to a authentic olive oil mill where you will discover the process of making extra virgin olive oil.

Homer called olive oil liquid gold. In ancient Greece, athletes ritually rubbed it all over their body. Its mystical glow illuminated history. Drops of it seeped into the bones of dead saints and martyrs through holes in their tombs. Olive oil has been more than mere food to the peoples of the mediterranean: it has been medicinal and the fountain of great wealth and power. Olive oil’s use in so many dishes and its mounting health benefits make it a must in home pantries and while it’s now possible to get good olive oil from places as far flung, olive oils from italy’s tuscany region remain the ones by which all others are measured.

After the visit to the olive trees yards and to the mill, you will finally taste the real extra virgin tuscan oil! After each visit and tasting you will have the chance to buy the wine and the oil at the farm and mill shop. And just then you will be picked up to your accommodation again.

All over the world food enthusiasts appreciate tuscan cuisine. Tuscan dishes are delicious and they are cooked using natural herbs, extra virgin olive oil, vegetables, fish, meat and obviously pasta. If are in Tuscany and relish these dishes, you will surely try to learn the essence of tuscan cuisine and if you have only a few spare hours to spend, our cooking school will teach you what you exactly want to learn in a single session cooking course.

The single session cooking courses are of two kinds: introductory class and immersion class. In all our cooking classes the chef is a well-known chef who studied on Pellegrino Artusi’s book.

My Fair Italy® can arrange a private driver to pick you up directly at your accommodation in Tuscany and bring you to the school where you can choose to spend an half day experience or a full day one. At the end of the course you will eat the things you made, and for most of people this is a real satisfaction! You can even choose to attend a cooking course longer than just one day. You can have a one week course with sessions in the mornings and in the afternoons.

You do not need any pot or saucepan. You will find everything you need directly in your school kitchen. On request we can also organize a tour to the market in order to make you see and understand the ideas that lies behind the word “food” for italians.
