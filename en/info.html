travel contracts and responsability

Travel contracts are under the law 27.12.77 n.1084 of the International Convention about travels (C.C.V.) signed in Bruxelles on 23rd April 1970, and under the directive 90/314/CE and under the Decree Law 17.03.95 n.111 on actuation n. 90/314/CE regarding travels, holidays and the all inclusive activities.

beginning and end of stay

In the price list for each room or apartment accommodation has been reported the entry and the exit time within which the stay must start and end. Normally the accommodations are available from 7 p.m. of the entrance day to 10 a.m. of the departure day. If any problem occur to be later than the days or time indicated, we immediately request to inform our agency or directly the estate or the hotel booked.

the accomodations

We have selected different kind of accommodations, such as: farmhouses, holiday homes and hotels. Selections and descriptions have been carried out with personal checks and visits, made up directly with the estate owners or the hotel managers, should any change occur after this publication and not known by us, must be immediately pointed out.

safe holidays

The client can pay the optional enrolment fee at the reservation time, which includes a special insurance policy for cancellation guarantee, luggage and assistance. All the conditions are detailed on the Insurance Policy page.

in the event of problems

In the event of difficulties or problems during the stay, we urge you to contact immediately our agency on the spot. It is the only way we can attend with a prompt action. Complaints will not be accepted on the return as for duties pertaining to our office and where necessary we will be at the guest disposal. Of course it is excluded everything caused by circumstances beyond our control (for further information see on page of General Conditions).

the excursions

Excursions are on payment. Assistants lead guests along the selected trip making them known about all the details and providing to give them all the information needed. Booking can be made by your agency at the stay reservation time or on the spot by our office every Monday morning. Special week-ends or tours can be booked only in advance in our agency. There is a minimum number of participants for every excursion so time by time each of one will be reconfirmed.
